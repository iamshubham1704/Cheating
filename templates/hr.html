Follow-up Questions

Behavioral Questions
  • Can you tell me about a time when you had to work under pressure?
  • Describe a situation where you had to solve a complex problem.
  • How do you handle stress in the workplace?
  • Tell me about a time you had to work with a difficult team member.
Technical Deep Dive
  • Can you walk me through your thought process for [specific technical problem]?
  • How would you approach debugging this issue?
  • What's your experience with [relevant technology]?
  • Can you explain this concept in simple terms?
Scenario-Based
  • If you were given this project, how would you start?
  • What would you do if you disagreed with your manager's decision?
  • How would you prioritize multiple urgent tasks?
  • Describe how you would handle a client complaint.
Clarification Questions
  • Can you elaborate on that point?
  • What specific challenges did you face?
  • How did you measure success in that project?
  • What would you do differently next time?
